Welcome to Avo Fitness App (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

Personal Data

While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to, your email address, name, or other details you provide when setting up your profile.

Camera and Image Data for Pose Detection

Our App uses your device's camera to provide real-time fitness coaching and exercise analysis through pose detection technology. To do this, the App requires access to your camera.

  • Real-time Processing: The video stream from your camera is processed in real-time on your device to analyze your body movements and provide feedback.
  • No Cloud Storage of Videos: We do not record, store, or transmit your video stream to our servers or any third-party servers. The analysis is performed locally on your device.
  • Data from Pose Detection: We only collect the resulting pose data (e.g., coordinates of body joints) which is anonymized and used to improve our application's features and accuracy. This data is not personally identifiable.

Usage Data

We may also collect information that your device sends whenever you use our App ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, operating system version, the features of our App that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those features, and other statistics.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide you with personalized fitness feedback and tracking.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.
  • Improve the functionality and accuracy of our pose detection algorithms.
  • Respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service.
